At You Do You Yoga Studio we want to help support you in whatever way you need at whatever pace feels right.

Our mission is to guide you on your yoga journey. Our classes are accessible, open, and enlightening for everyone – no matter where you are in your practice. We want you to leave our classes feeling motivated, invigorated, and seriously impressed with yourself when you leave the studio, whether that’s while walking to your car or logging off of your computer.

Classes include yoga basics, slow flow, gentle yoga, power yoga, and more. There truly is something for everyone. We encourage you to try new things, but ultimately encourage you to do you.

Our instructors are trained to guide you through asanas (poses), prana (breathing techniques), and savasana (meditation).
