We truly believe that yoga is for everyone. There are so many different types of yoga out there, you just have to find what is right for you. That is why we offer a wide range of classes from basics to power yoga.

We offer a 30-day unlimited yoga pass for our new students, so you can try as many different classes and teachers as you can. We want you to find what’s right for you and will help you in whatever way we can.

• Back to Basics •

If words like 'downward dog,' 'vinyasa,' or 'chataranga' leave you feeling confused, then this is the class for you. This class will help you get started in the world of yoga through detailed instructions and beginner poses. It's a great refresher for existing students too.

Basic yoga poses, proper alignment, the benefits of proper breathing, use of props, breathing techniques, and relaxation are taught and explored.

This class is a great confidence booster for any level of practice - old or new to the yoga world. No heat. Beginners welcome.

• Gentle Yoga •

Stressed? We’ve got a class for that. This mellow class places special emphasis on the coordination of movement with breath and focuses on relaxing gentle movements, deep stretches, muscle release, and restorative postures to help your whole body untangle and unwind.

Props are often used to help support the body specifically on areas like shoulders, hips, hamstrings, and the spine. Postures are held much longer to help you tap into connective tissue, giving you a greater release.

No yoga experience required. All levels welcome - Ideal for beginners, seniors, and those with injuries or other physical limitations.

• Flow •

Looking for a yoga class that energizes while helping you to build strength and flexibility? Our Flow class is for you! Awaken your strength, self-awareness, and flexibility in a fun and empowering way.

Links breath to movement, creative sequences, and challenging yet accessible postures. Each Flow teacher has his/her own unique style so make sure to check out all our instructors to find your favorites!

Modifications offered for all experience levels.

• Slow Flow •

Similar to our Yoga Flow class, Slow Flow focuses on using our breath to connect with our bodies, mindful movements, and creative sequencing, but at a slightly slower pace. Flow through the practice to strengthen, detoxify, and re-energize your body.

Movements will be mild, sometimes held longer, with plenty of time for transitions and adjustments.

This gentle grounding practice is appropriate for all abilities. No heat.

• Power Flow •

Looking for something a little more intense? Power Flow combines Yoga with strength training. Staying connected to the mind, body, and breath this class pushes you to your limits.

Fast paced, flowing series. In the colder months at the studio we add a little heat to get you warmer faster. Approx. 85 degrees.

Vigorous practice. Yoga experience recommended.

• Power Hour •

Need to get in quickie? Power Hour is a great way to get in a quick workout in your day.

Fast paced, flowing series. Very similar to Power Flow, just a little shorter and sweatier. In the colder months at the studio we add a little heat to get you warmer faster. Approx. 85degrees.

Experienced required for this vigorous class.

Unsure which class is right for you? Give us a call, or shoot us an email, and we’d be happy to chat with you to make sure you’re in the right class.

Pricing good for physical studio and virtual studio.
