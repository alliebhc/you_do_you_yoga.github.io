• Paws for Yoga •

$12 | AUG. 26 | 6-7 PM | APA Adoption Center & Online

Join us for an hour-long yoga class in the lobby of the APA with some very special assistant yoga teachers, cats! Throughout the duration of class, our feline friends will have full reign of our make-shift yoga class, ensuring you leave class “feline good.” It’s the purrrrrrfect combination of yoga mats and kitty cats! If you meet your new best cat friend while in downward dog, remember - all cats are available for adoption. Space is limited to 20, so book your ticket "meow!"

If you want to join in virtually with your own cat (or dog!) you can register for an online ticket.

All proceeds will benefit the APA’s adoption program.

All levels | Please bring a yoga mat | Space is limited
